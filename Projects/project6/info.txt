Title - Call Center Customer Retention Dashboard (PWC Job Simulation)
Description - Developed for a telecom client during a PWC Power BI job simulation to visualize call center KPIs and customer retention drivers.
Detailed - <b>Case Scenario:</b><br>Executed as part of the PWC Power BI Job Simulation. The virtual client, a major Telecom provider, was facing high customer churn and needed transparency into their Call Center&apos;s performance to identify the root causes.<br><br><b>The Solution:</b><br>Designed an interactive Power BI dashboard to translate raw call logs into actionable retention strategies.<br><br><b>Key Deliverables:</b><ul><li><b>Operational Transparency:</b> Visualized 3-month call volume trends and resolution rates (72.92% resolved) to pinpoint service gaps.</li><li><b>Agent Performance Benchmarking:</b> Built granular matrices comparing agents on &apos;Speed of Answer&apos; vs. &apos;Satisfaction Rating&apos; to identify training needs.</li><li><b>Satisfaction Analysis:</b> Monitored Average Satisfaction (3.40) against wait times (67.52s) to validate the link between efficiency and loyalty.</li></ul><p><b>Tools &amp; Impact:</b> Used Power BI and DAX measures to provide management with the insights needed to reduce wait times and improve agent resolution protocols.</p>
