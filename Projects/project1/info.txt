Title - Automated Community Engagement & Analytics Ecosystem
Description - End-to-end automation to onboard new members, segment audiences by interest, and drive higher event attendance using data analytics.
Detailed - <b>Project Overview:</b><br>Designed a fully automated onboarding pipeline to replace manual registration. The system captures granular member preferences to help leadership plan high-impact events.<br><br><b>The Solution Architecture:</b><ul><li><b>Granular Data Capture:</b> Uses a smart Microsoft Form for new registrations and collects specific gaming preferences at source, distinguishing between casual and competitive players, preferred Genre.</li><li><b>Automated Provisioning:</b> A Power Automate flow instantly processes new registrations and adds members to relevant Microsoft Teams channels based on their interests. Processes Preference Data into PowerBi Data Model </li><li><b>Engagement Analytics:</b> Feeds data into Power BI to visualize trends (e.g., PC vs Mobile gamers), enabling data-driven decisions for future event/tournament planning.</li><li><b>Impact:</b> Reduced manual data entry by 95 percent and significantly improved event turnout by 60% aligning activities with actual member demand.</li></ul><p><b>Tech Stack:</b> Power Automate, Microsoft Forms, Power BI, Microsoft Teams.</p>
