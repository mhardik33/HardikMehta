Title - HR Diversity & Inclusion Dashboard (PWC Job Simulation)
Description - Strategic HR dashboard developed during a PWC job simulation to visualize gender balance, turnover, and performance KPIs.
Detailed - <b>Case Scenario:</b><br>Executed as part of the PWC Power BI Job Simulation for a telecom client struggling with gender balance at the executive level. The objective was to diagnose root causes for the lack of diversity progress.<br><br><b>Key Insights & Analysis:</b><ul><li><b>Root Cause Analysis:</b> Discovered that while average performance ratings were identical for men and women (2.49), female turnover was higher (10.24% vs 8.81%), indicating retention issues rather than performance gaps.</li><li><b>Pipeline Leaks:</b> Visualized hiring trends (41% Female) and promotion disparities to pinpoint where the talent pipeline was breaking down.</li><li><b>Departmental Segregation:</b> Highlighted structural imbalances, identifying that departments like HR were female-dominated while Strategy and Operations remained male-heavy.</li></ul><p><b>Tools & Impact:</b> Used Power BI to transform raw HR data into a strategic asset, enabling the client to shift focus from general recruitment to targeted retention and inclusive culture initiatives.</p>
